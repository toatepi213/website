The Impact of Vaping on Your Body

Vaping might seem like a cool trend, but it's important to know how it really affects your body. While it may feel harmless, vaping has serious side effects, especially on the lungs, heart, and brain. Teenagers are still growing, and vaping can interfere with that development, leading to long-term health problems.

It's not just about physical health either—vaping can affect your mood and concentration, making it harder to focus on schoolwork and stay motivated.

Vaping and Academic Performance

Did you know that vaping can also impact your grades? Many students who vape report having trouble concentrating, remembering things, and staying alert in class. Studies have shown that nicotine and other chemicals in vapes can harm the brain's ability to function properly, especially when it comes to learning and memory.

If you want to stay ahead in your studies, avoiding vaping is a smart move. Your brain is your biggest asset—protect it!

Why Quit Vaping?

Quitting vaping is tough, but it's worth it! When you stop vaping, you'll start to notice improvements in your energy, focus, and overall mood. Many teenagers who quit say they feel more motivated and do better at school. Plus, you’re investing in a healthier future.

Support and Resources

If you're thinking about quitting or want to learn more, there are resources available to help. Talk to someone you trust, or check out the support options right here in South Auckland. You're not alone, and getting help is a sign of strength.
